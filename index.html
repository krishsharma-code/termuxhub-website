About Termux Hub

Termux Hub is an educational initiative dedicated to bringing high-quality cybersecurity learning resources to mobile platforms. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to learn ethical hacking and network security, regardless of whether they own a high-end laptop or computer.

Our mission is to empower students and enthusiasts to practice professional tools like Nmap, Metasploit, and Sherlock directly on their Android devices using Termux. All content is strictly for educational and ethical purposes.
